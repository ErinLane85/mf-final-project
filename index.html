Erin Lane

  • 1985
    Birth Year

    On June 25, 1985 in Soldotna, Alaska, Chris Lane went into labor and had no idea she'd be coming home with twins. After hours of labor and lots of hospital staff, Erin and Katrina Lane were born 7 minutes apart. They were the talk of the small town!

  • 2003
    High School Graduation

    After years of school and lots of memories made, Erin and Katrina graduated high school. They're both ready and excited for new adventures in college. Katrina was headed up north to Humboldt and Erin was headed west to Monterey Bay.

  • 2011
    College Graduation

    7 years of college, 3 degrees earned, countless classes and over 10 countries visited, college is complete! It was a wild ride and so much fun! The memories and experiences will last a lifetime. The future is up to me and I intend on making the most of every moment.

  • 2022
    Present Day

    11 years later and I'm still enjoying the ride. These years haven't been without struggle, loss, heartache and tears but they've also been filled with endless laughs, memories and experiences. I'm thankful for everything and excited for everything to come.
