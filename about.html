Resume

On June 25, 1985, I was born in Soldotna, Alaska. I have a twin sister and two older brothers. We only spent a year in Alaska and then we moved back to California where we grew up. We spent most of our time in Sacramento but we did live in the Bay Area for a couple of crazy years. I graduated high school in 2003 and went straight to college.

I started my college career at Monterey Bay State University. After a year away, I decided to come back to Sacramento and go to community college and take as many classes as possible to learn as much as I could. After a few years, I transferred over to Sacramento State University where I finished up my college career. While at Sac State, I had the opportunity to study in Hungary and Dominican Republic. While I lived in Hungary, I was able to travel to numerous different countries and gain a wealth of experiences. I finally graduated in 2011 with my Bachelor's of Science in Marketing, International Business and Entrepreneurship.

Once I graduated from college, my first job was as VP of Operations running a nonprofit organization. Though I loved my job, I was called to go on a missions trip to Ghana, West Africa. When I returned, I got a job working as the Finance Director at my church. I spent 3 years at the church before I went to work with the State Treasurer's office working on loans, bonds and grants. I enjoyed my job, but I missed the nonprofit work, so I went back to working with two different nonprofit organizations as an accounting specialist and event planner. It wasn't long after that, I decided to venture out and work for myself. I have been working as a Notary Public and Loan Signing Agent ever since. I love working for myself but it didn't take me long to recognize that having multiple sources of income is best. Which is what lead me to BitWize and learning about webdesign and coding.
